Margin vs. Border vs. Padding

062115

Try to imagine the image above as a castle. The contents of the castle, is the king. The padding that surrounds the content (king in our case),are the king's knights that basically form a protective barrier around him. The more guards he has protecting him, the thicker the padding. The border serves as the castle walls. The thicker you make the border, the thicker the walls. Lastly, the margin, being the area surrounding the border, could be thought of as a moat surrounding the castle itself.
